SAINTPREMO

Mark John Ignacio Guillermo (born June 22, 2003), better known by his artist name SaintPremo, is an independent Filipino artist of both music and visual art.

From a young age, his innate talent for drawing and passion for the visual arts were evident. Countless hours in his childhood were dedicated to doodling imaginative ideas onto paper.
